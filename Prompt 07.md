**| Risk                                    | Level | Mitigation                                                 |**

**|-----------------------------------------|-------|------------------------------------------------------------|**

**| Inaccurate loan details in the email (e.g., wrong loan amount or terms) | High  | Validate all loan details before running the prompt.        |**

**| Impersonal tone in the email           | Medium | Include personalized elements (e.g., applicant’s name) for a warmer tone. |**

**| Automation of sensitive information might lead to privacy risks | High  | Ensure secure email systems and compliance with data privacy regulations. |**



**\*\*Overall risk rating\*\*: Medium — The prompt is effective but requires human oversight to ensure the correct data is used and the tone is appropriate.**
