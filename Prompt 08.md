### **## ❗ \*\*Problem Being Solved\*\***



**Drafting rejection letters manually can be time-consuming and may lack empathy or professionalism. This prompt automates the process, ensuring consistent, polite, and empathetic communication with applicants.**



**\*\*Pain points addressed:\*\***

**- Time spent drafting rejection letters.**

**- Variability in tone and format of rejection communication.**

**- The potential for insensitive or impersonal responses.**

### **## ⚠️ \*\*Risks and Limitations\*\***



**| Risk                                    | Level | Mitigation                                                 |**

**|-----------------------------------------|-------|------------------------------------------------------------|**

**| Impersonal tone in rejection letter    | High  | Use personalized details (e.g., applicant’s name, situation) for a warmer tone. |**

**| Incomplete or inaccurate rejection reasons | Medium | Ensure all relevant reasons for rejection are considered before finalizing the letter. |**

**| Sending the letter too quickly without reviewing | Medium | Include a final review step before sending the rejection letter. |**



**\*\*Overall risk rating\*\*: Medium — The process is efficient but requires careful review to ensure empathy and accuracy.**
